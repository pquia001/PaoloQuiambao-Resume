Paolo Quiambao

Business Economics

Paolo Quiambao was raised in Panorama City, Los Angeles by a hardworking mother and father. His passion has always been dancing, but has since moved on from this hobby to pursue work in the world of business. By the age of 15, Paolo has already successfully started several businesses with the help of his family.

Owning multiple six-figure businesses, Paolo has achieved what his parents migrated to the United States for, a stable and wealthy life that will be able to be passed down generation to generation. Over the years, Paolo has continued to accumulate properties that provide cash flow and prosperity for his loved ones.

Currently, Paolo is working on starting another business. He plans to dive into the world of fashion and immerse himself in the vast opportunities that were given to him because of his determination to make a mark in the fashion industry. With the help of friends and family, Paolo plans to release his first line of clothing in the summer of 2022.
